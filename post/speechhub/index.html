So today I found Speechhub, and after modifying it a little, I have my new Shorts-like blog up on Github.

It's pretty basic, but that's the idea. I've taken the concept behind Svbtle as a spiritual guide to how I will approach blogging, and we'll see where it goes. And of course, I'll be fixing up how this place looks as time goes on.
